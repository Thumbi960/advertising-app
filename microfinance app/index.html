Our Services

Fire Safety Survey

Our fire safety services include fire risk assessments, installation of fire suppression systems, fire drills, and compliance with fire safety regulations. We work with businesses and organizations to enhance fire prevention measures and response capabilities.

Security Consultancy

Our security consultancy services are designed to help businesses assess risks, develop security strategies, and implement effective protective measures. We provide tailored solutions, including threat assessments, security audits, and emergency response planning.

Hazardous Material Handling

We provide expert solutions for the identification, handling, transportation, storage, and disposal of hazardous materials. Our services ensure compliance with regulatory standards while minimizing risks to human health and the environment..

Training & Awareness Program

We offer customized training sessions and awareness programs to educate employees and individuals on safety protocols, risk mitigation strategies, and emergency preparedness. Our programs cover a wide range of safety topics and are designed to meet industry-specific requirements.

First Aid

We provide professional first aid training and services to equip individuals and businesses with essential life-saving skills. Our courses cover CPR, emergency response, and workplace first aid, ensuring readiness to handle medical emergencies effectively. .

Waste Management

Our waste management solutions focus on responsible waste disposal, recycling initiatives, and sustainable practices. We help organizations manage their waste efficiently while adhering to environmental regulations and promoting sustainability..

Security Risk Analysis

We help organizations to identify and understand the security risks to their business. We build business resilience by mitigating and governing identified risks. Our service offering spans an end-to-end range of integrated strategic consultancy, expert analysis, audit, security management, security operations, investigative, project management, travel risk management, digital tracking and monitoring, and crisis response services.
