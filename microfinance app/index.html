OUR SERVICES

Business Loans

We are a business credit facility that allows a business to borrow against its future sales. Inspire Credit Limited gives you a lump sum of money, and then you give us a portion of your daily or weekly sales until you clear the loan.
Logbook Loans

This is a type of loan that uses your motor vehicle as security – a car, van, tractor, or motorbike. It allows you to borrow an agreed amount from the lender against the value of your vehicle.
Salary Loans

We allow you to borrow money for your future paycheck. Essentially, you get your salary in advance. You can use the money to cover an emergency, like car repairs, and pay it back when you get paid.
